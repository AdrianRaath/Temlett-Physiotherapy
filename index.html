Our Approach

Individualised Physiotherapy Care

Treatment tailored to your injury, goals, and lifestyle

At Temlett Physiotherapy, we offer comprehensive, individualised care tailored to your injury, and goals. Our approach is dynamic and evidence-based combining education, hands-on treatment and active rehabilitation to assist in your recovery.

We treat a range of neuromuscularskeletal conditions, assist with acute and chronic injuries and provide structured post-operative rehabilitation. Our integrated approach means we work closely with medical doctors, surgeons, coaches, biokineticists and other allied healthcare professions to ensure your recovery is safe, appropriately progressed and supported from every angle.

Neuromusculoskeletal & Orthopaedic Conditions

We treat a variety of conditions affecting muscles, tendons, joints, ligaments and nerves including:

  • Back and neck pain
  • Disc-related conditions, sciatica and nerve pathologies
  • Headache and jaw pain
  • Joint pain and arthritis
  • Tendon and ligament injuries
  • Overuse injuries
  • Pre- and post-surgical rehabilitation

Pre & Post-operative Physiotherapy

At Temlett Physiotherapy we prepare your body for surgery and guide your rehabilitation afterward. Pre-operative treatment focuses on building strength and confidence, while post-operative care includes pain management, restoring movement, rebuilding strength, and facilitating a safe return to daily life, work, and sport - following your surgeon's protocol.

Sports Injuries & Rehabilitation

Whether you train for fun or competition, injury can be disruptive and frustrating. We provide thorough assessment and evidence-based management to help you return to activity safely and efficiently.

We have a special interest in paediatric and adolescent injuries, supporting young athletes through growth-related conditions or injuries and encouraging healthy participation in sport.

In addition, we have extensive experience in assessing and treating running and dance-related injuries, providing targeted care for athletes of all levels.

Rehabilitation includes improving function through exercise by enhancing strength and endurance, balance, agility, power and cardiovascular fitness.

Amputation Rehabilitation

A particular passion at Temlett Physiotherapy is providing care for amputees - from post-operative recovery to prosthetic training and return to activity or sport.

Our approach includes:

  • Pain neuroscience education, particularly for those suffering from phantom limb pain
  • Strength and mobility exercises
  • Gait retraining
  • Prosthetic integration
About

Meet Your Physiotherapist

Caro Temlett

Caro is a compassionate Cape Town-based physiotherapist with a special interest in sports and orthopaedic rehabilitation. She completed her BSc in Physiotherapy at the University of Cape Town in 2018 (with distinction) and her community service at a regional hospital in Gauteng, before working in private practice in Cape Town for the past six years.

She holds a Postgraduate Diploma in Interdisciplinary Pain Management (UCT, 2021, with distinction), which strengthened her interest in evidence-based pain management for acute and chronic conditions. Caro recently completed her MSc in Sport and Exercise Physiotherapy at UCT in 2026 (with distinction). Her research focused on lower-limb injuries and proprioception in professional ballet dancers, in collaboration with Cape Town City Ballet.

With a strong personal background in movement, including ballet, teaching clinical Pilates (BASI Matwork certified), and completing multiple road and trail half marathons, Caro brings a genuine love of movement into her rehabilitation approach, helping people return confidently to the activities they care about.
